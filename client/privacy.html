Privacy Policy

Welcome to HiAirDrop. We value your privacy and personal information protection. This privacy policy aims to explain how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

Our service is based on peer-to-peer (P2P) technology, which does not collect, store, or access the content of the files you transmit. We may collect the following information:

  • Basic usage data (such as access time, usage duration, etc.)
  • Device information (such as browser type, operating system, etc.)
  • Network connection information (for establishing P2P connections)

2. Information Use

We collect information solely for:

  • Providing and improving our service
  • Ensuring the security of the service
  • Analyzing service usage
  • Optimizing user experience

3. P2P Transmission Security

We use WebRTC technology to implement end-to-end file transmission:

  • Files are directly transmitted between devices, without passing through our servers
  • Transmission uses an encrypted channel
  • We cannot access or view the content of the transmitted files

4. Cookie Usage

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve user experience
  • Collect usage statistics

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Information Security

We take multiple security measures to protect your information:

  • Use encryption technology to protect data transmission
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Restrict internal access permissions

6. Information Sharing

We will not sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal information, unless:

  • You have given us your explicit authorization
  • Legal requirements
  • Protecting our legitimate rights and interests

7. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and obtain your information
  • Correct or delete your information
  • The right to revoke consent
  • The right to complain

8. Privacy Policy Update

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on the website, and the update date will be noted. We recommend that you check this page regularly to view the latest changes.
